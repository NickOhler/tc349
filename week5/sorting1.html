Process and Findings

The architecture of the site was relatively close to what was defined by my sort, as well as the sort determined by the participants. I found that my sort happened pretty quickly, while my participants took their time to finally decide on the groups that they wanted to stick with.

My sort did differ slightly from the sort that my participants made. The participants left a couple pieces of content alone, to be a group of their own. This resulted in my sort have one less category. The participants had one large 'buying' group, which included everything product related. My sort included both a 'promotional' and 'product buying' categories
